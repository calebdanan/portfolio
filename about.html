Caleb Nguyen
Concept Artist

As a passionate and innovative concept artist, Caleb brings imagination to life through captivating visuals and storytelling. With a keen eye for detail and a flair for creativity, he transform ideas into stunning visual representations that breathe life into the worlds of entertainment and beyond.

From The School of the Art Institute of Chicago, Caleb has honed his skills in the dynamic realm of concept artistry. Drawing inspiration from a diverse range of influences, he blend technical proficiency with a unique artistic vision to create compelling and immersive designs.

About Me
With a successful track record in the industry, Caleb has collaborated with the likes of M.J. Hegar's Digital Team in her run for U.S. Senate; an Artist in Residency for H.E.B. creating large-scale murals; and a commercial artist and book illustrator, to bring forth visually striking concepts that set the stage for memorable experiences.

Professional Journey
Along the way, Caleb worked for three years as a Starbucks Barista, having worked in the highest-profit store in downtown Chicago. Managers and collaborators have given high praise for the fast pace, quick to collaborate and communicate, and thoughtful problem-solving. His portfolio spans across traditional fine arts to different variations of digital art, showcasing versatility and adaptability in meeting the demands of each project. Professors have commended Caleb for my dedication to detail, easy-going nature, and creativity on projects of which have been told, "have never been seen in any of my classes."

Experience
